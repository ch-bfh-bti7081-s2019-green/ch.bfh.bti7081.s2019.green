## Depression

- Episodes longer than 2 weeks
- Symptoms
  * Loss of interest
  * Loss of confidence
  * Pain
  * Suicide
- Treatment
  * Anti-Depressants
  * Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  * InterPersonal Psychotherapy (IPT)
